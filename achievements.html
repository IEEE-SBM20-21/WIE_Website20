OUR PROJECTS

WIE WEBSITE

The team of the WIE Affinity Group of IEEE Student Branch Manipal undertook the project of designing a website for WIE IEEE- SBM in the month of September The team consisted of a total of 10 members who worked under the guidance of 4 board members from IEEE SBM. The website was designed using CSS, Html and JavaScript. The webdev team had 8 members. They learned the different aspects ie graphics and web development from scratch and ultimately delivered great results. Each of them took up the task of designing the different pages.
There was also a dedicated team for content who provided the articles for the WIE blog and other content material for the different pages of the website. It was a great learning opportunity for all the members as they got to learn and build on the skills of website development and team work.
We would like to thank the webmaster, Abhishek Srivastav and graphics head, Ashish Sharma of IEEE SBM for their guidance and support in this project and the Chairperson and Vice Chairperson of WIE Affinty Group Navya Ambardar and Prita Saha for taking the initiative.
