IEEE

IEEE is a global technical professional organization dedicated to nurture technological innovations and advancements that would help in improving global conditions and shape a better life. It is a community of professionals who collaborate and work for advancement and support young minds join them and broaden their knowledge. ABOUT IEEE WIE

WIE is a network connecting IEEE members and volunteers at a global scale to promote women in the fields of engineering and science that aims at encouraging women professionals to have a lasting career.
